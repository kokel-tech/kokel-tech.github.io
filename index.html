About Us

We are a group of IT professionals with years of experience in the industry. Kokel Tech was founded not only to make productive use of our downtime but also to assist in the digitalization of businesses at every level, from small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) to large corporations.

Don't worry if you lack experience in the IT world but want to create a digital product. We will assist you through every step, from user interface, user experience, and designing data model that helping you build a database that can enhance your business performance.

In addition to developing mobile apps and websites, we also have a dedicated data team ready to help you with consultations for creating AI-based products.
